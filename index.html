Eastern Kenya integrated college (Ekic) is a joint venture of the Africa Brotherhood Church,

Canadian Baptist mission and carey Theological college of the University of British Columbia. It first begun

on 12th february, 1950 as Divinity school,

it finally evolved to Africa integrated Theological college (AITC) and later to the present day

Eastern Kenya Integraed College. it is fully registered college by the ministry of education

whose teacher education courses, business and Technical coarses are examined by KNEC, NITA, KASNEB, ICM & ABE

among others

Location

Eastern Kenya Integrated college is located at Mitaboni in

Kathiani constituency formerly Machakos District; 63km from Nairobi city and 13km off the Nairobi-kangundo road

and 25km from Machakos town.
